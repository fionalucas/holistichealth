About me

For years I suffered with poor body image and yo-yo dieting. I hated my body and was never happy with it, no matter how much weight I lost or new plan I tried. I punished myself and lived in a cycle of dieting, restriction and bingeing. I also never knew my purpose and thought there was nothing I was good at. I suffered alone and in silence. My heart was heavy, my soul was sad and I couldn’t even look myself in the eye in the mirror. Now that I live in alignment with my true self I have found my healthy balanced lifestyle, discovered my life passion in helping others, created a positive mindset and body image, and I finally truely love and accept myself. And it is a game changer. I am the happiest and healthiest I have ever been. I have found a beautiful peace and I continue to grow, explore and experience life with fresh eyes and a light heart. It is my purpose to help others stop suffering and become more healthy and happy from the inside out.

QUALIFICATIONS - Holistic Wellness Coach - Nature Care College, Sydney. Specialising in nutrition, self care, natural therapies, emotional and spiritual wellness, food as medicine, weight management.
